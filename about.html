About Raffles Voices

Raffles Voices (RV) is a Co-Curricular Activity in Raffles Institution, dedicated to developing the finest singing voices in Singapore. Raffles Voices was founded in 1992 by music teacher Ms Charmaine Chiang, and has since gone on to great heights under the baton of conductor Mr Toh Ban Sheng.
